TIKE SMART Pebbles Bath Mat - Safety Meets Style

The TIKE SMART Pebbles Bath Mat is the perfect blend of safety and comfort for your bathroom. Designed for smooth floors, its non-slip feature ensures that you can walk confidently, even when wet. With an oval size of 27x14 inches, it fits most standard bathtubs and showers, making it a versatile choice for any bathroom setup.

This mat is equipped with 104 reinforced suction cups that keep it securely in place, offering stability for users of all ages.
